Clean Mealz Oats


Why We Love This Meal.


Over night oats are largely responsible for getting me out of bed in the morning. I love my bed and I need a very good reason to get out of it. This breakfast always does the trick. Since these are over night oats, there's no cooking involved. You just mix everything together the night before and the rest takes care of itself. It makes for a great start to the days.


This is a super easy recipe to follow along with. Whether your an experienced cook or new in the kitchen, be sure to give this recipe a try!

Everything You Need To Know.

Ingredients List

  • 240ml Oats
  • 60g Porridge Oats
  • 30g Frozen Raspberries
  • 1 tbsp of Chia Seeds
  • 1 tbsp of Nuts
  • 1 tsp of Agave Syrup

Step By Step Guide

  1. Combine the milk, oats,raspberries and chia seeds in a large bowl.
  2. Transfer the mixture into a serving bowl.
  3. Cover and leave to soak over night in the fridge.
  4. Next morning, top with nuts and agave syrup and serve.

Clean Mealz Very Berry Smoothie


Why We Love This Meal.


This is a great smoothie to have in the morning or after a workout. The chia seeds are full of omega-3, which is great news for your heart. Chia seeds can absorb up to 10 times they're weight in water which works wonders in your digestive system. Make sure you drink the smoothie as soon as you make it, we don't want the seeds to swell up too much! If the smoothie is going to be your only breakfast, make it more substantial by adding a protein powder of your choice!


Everything You Need To Know.

Ingredients List

  • 300 ml Apple Juice
  • 1 ripe Banana (peeled)
  • 100g of Mixed Frozen Berries
  • 1 tbsp of Chia Seeds

Step By Step Guide

  1. Pour the apple juice into a blender and add the banana berries and chia seeds. in a large bowl.
  2. Blitz until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a small glass and serve without delay.

Clean Mealz Poached Eggs On Avocado and Feta Toast


Why We Love This Meal.


This recipe is a moreish combination of creamy avocado, salty feta, a hint of chilli, topped off with a runny poached egg. Worth getting up for!
It's a great option for a post workout meal, packed full of macronutrients like protein and fat, it's an excellent meal to kickstart your recovery process.


Everything You Need To Know.

Ingredients List

  • 2 tbsp White wine vinegar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • 50g feta
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • juice of half a lemon
  • 2 slices of sourdough

Step By Step Guide

  1. Fill a large saucepan with water, vinegar and bring to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, reduce the heat so the water is simmering, then swirl the water into a whirlpool. Crack the eggs and cook for 2 1/2 mins.
  3. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, mash the avocado, feta, chilli flakes and lemon juice, and season with black pepper. Toast the bread.
  4. Pile the avocado and feta on the toast, then put a poached egg on top of each.

Macros Amount
Protein 20g
Fats 31g
Carbs 37g
Fibre 6g
Cal 517 kcal

Clean Mealz Three Bean Salad


Why We Love This Meal.


It's a smart move to have plenty of tinned beans in your cupboard. Beans are economical and they keep good for ages.
They're complex foods that are rich in nutrients. Beans take a long time to cook from scratch, so high quality tinned beans do just fine.
This salad makes for a filling lunch with a nice spicy kick. It keeps really well, even with the dressing on. Making it an ideal workday lunch.

Everything You Need To Know.

Ingredients List

  • 400g tinned mixed beans
  • 80g of rinsed and dried sweetcorn
  • 3 scallions, finely diced
  • 1 finely chopped red pepper.
  • a hanful of corriander
  • For the Dressing
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  • a few drops of Tabasco
  • Quarter tsp chilli flakes

Step By Step Guide

  1. Place beans, sweetcorn, scallions and red pepper in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Place all the ingredients needed for the dressing in a jar and shake to combine.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad.
  4. Sprinkle corriander leaves over the salad and serve.

nutritional Info

Macros Amount
Protein 17.2g
Fats 15.5g
Carbs 45g
Fibre 3.7g
Cal 395 kcal

Clean Mealz Tuna Pesto Pasta


Why We Love This Meal.


This is a real student lunch - in the nicest possible sense. In university, you're always on the go. This tuna pesto pasta could be your next lunch staple. It will always set you up for a busy afternoon wheter with work, the gym or other activities. The ingredients are super simple to fid and combine into a beautifully nutritious lunch. Tuna is packed dull of omega-3 and the pasta will give you the energy you need for the afternoon ahead. This dish is delicious cooked fresh and served hot, but the left overs are just as tasty as cold pasta salad. A no fuss tasty lunch if there ever was one.

Everything You Need To Know.

Ingredients List

  • 80g wholegrain pasta
  • 112g tin of tuna in olive oil, drained.
  • 2 tbsp pesto
  • A small handful of chopped scallions.
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan

Step By Step Guide

  1. Cook the pasta based on the instructions of the packet.
  2. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
  3. Stir in the tuna, pesto and scallions and heat through.
  4. scrape the pasta into a warm serving bowl.
  5. Sprinkle over the parmesan and serve.

Fats 39g
Carbs 51g
Fibre 8.4g

Clean Mealz Broccoli and Feta Salad.


Why We Love This Meal.


This is a tasty high fibre salad. The raw broccoli and peppers have a lovely crunch that's offset by the feta, and the chickpeas pack in some extra protein, complex carbohydrates and fiber. If you want to increase the carbohydrates serve with some wholegrain pitta on the side. This is a salad that is easy enough to pull together in under 10 minutes, but nice enough to serve to friends.

Everything You Need To Know.

Ingredients List

  • 400g of broccoli florets chopped into bite size pieces.
  • 400g tin of chickpeas, drained and rinsed.
  • 2 peppers, deseeded and diced.
  • 75g of feta
  • For The Dressing
  • 2 garlic gloves, crushed
  • 4 tbsp natural yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Step By Step Guide

  1. Place the broccoli, chickpeas and feta in a large serving bowl and mix well.
  2. Place all of your ingredients for the dressing in a small bowl and stir well.
  3. When you are ready to serve the salad pour the dressing over and toss well.

Clean Mealz Chicken, Pasta nand Pea Broth


Why We Love This Meal.


This is one of the quickest evening meals you can make. I'll reach for this when I come home tired, or just after a hard workout when I want something simple but soothing. It's a really adaptable recipe, so don't be afraid to experiment. Try adding a little smoked bacon and parsley or thyme. The choices are trully endless.

Everything You Need To Know.

Ingredients List

  • 1 tbsp olive oil.
  • 2 skinless chicken breast fillets finely sliced.
  • 100g spelt pasta.
  • 500ml chicken stock.
  • A handful of chopped scallions.
  • a handful of basil leaves, chopped.

Step By Step Guide

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the chicken until cooked through.
  3. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to packet instructions (with chicken stock)
  4. Let the pasta and stock simmer together.
  5. Add in cooked chicken, frozen peas and scallions and bring to the boil.
  6. Cokk for about 3 minutes until chicken and veg are heated through.
  7. Prepare in serving bowls and sprinkle over basil leaves.

Clean Mealz Lasagne


Why We Love This Meal.


Lasagne us usually a meal that I usually endulge in when I'm out for a meal with friends. At home, I make a leaner version. Using only a small amout of butter and cheese, and using spelt flour instead of white flour. Don't worry, it's still delicious. When you make this, don't be afraid to make two batches, it's great to put in the freezer, no matter how busy you get you'll have Clean Mealz Lasagne ready for you.

Everything You Need To Know.

Ingredients List

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 900g lean minced beef
  • 1 quiantity of your prefeered tomato sauce.
  • A handful of basil leaves, chopped.
  • 150g wholewheat lasagne sheets
  • 1 quntity of your preferred Cheese Sauce

Step By Step Guide

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 celcius/Gas 4. You will need a large lasagne dish.
  2. Heat the oil in a large casserole over a medium heat. Add the mince and cook for about 10 minutes, until brown. Stir in the tomato sauce and basil and set aside.
  3. Pour half the mince into the lasagne dish. Top with a lasagne sheet.
  4. Pour in the rest of the mince and top with a second layer of lasgane sheet.
  5. Cook the dish in the oven for 20 minutes.
